Overview

ShopUnix BG Cleaner respects your privacy. This app is designed to let users remove image backgrounds quickly and securely. We do not collect personal data or upload your images to any server.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information or media files.

Permissions

To function properly, the app requests access to:

  • Photos and Media (READ_MEDIA_IMAGES): This is used only to let you choose images from your gallery for background removal. Images are processed locally on your device and are not uploaded or stored.

On supported devices, we also use the Android Photo Picker API to allow private and secure media selection without needing full gallery access.

Image Processing

All image editing and background removal happens locally on your device. No image data is transmitted to external servers.
