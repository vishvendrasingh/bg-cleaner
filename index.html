Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 22, 2025

Overview

ShopUnix BG Cleaner is committed to protecting your privacy. This app uses artificial intelligence to remove image backgrounds quickly and securely. We collect only the photo data necessary for processing and do not share your images with any third parties.

Information We Collect

We collect and process the following data:

  • Photo Data: Images you select for background removal are temporarily processed using our AI technology to remove backgrounds.
  • No Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers.

How We Use Your Data

Your photo data is used exclusively for:

  • Processing images through our AI background removal technology
  • Providing you with the edited image with background removed
  • Improving our AI algorithms for better background removal quality

Data Storage and Security

We take data security seriously:

  • Images are processed securely using industry-standard encryption
  • All data transmission is encrypted to protect your privacy

Third-Party Sharing

We do not share, sell, or distribute your photo data to any third parties. Your images remain private and are used solely for the background removal service you requested.

Permissions

To function properly, the app requests access to:

  • Photos and Media (READ_MEDIA_IMAGES): This allows you to select images from your gallery for background removal processing.
  • Internet Access: Required to send images to our AI processing servers and receive the edited results.

On supported devices, we use the Android Photo Picker API for secure media selection.

Data Retention

Images are processed and temporarily stored on our servers as required by legal and operational requirements. After this period, all copies of your photos are permanently deleted from our servers. We do not retain images longer than necessary for legal compliance and service delivery.

Children's Privacy

ShopUnix BG Cleaner does not target children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has used our service, we will immediately delete any associated data.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Know what data we process (only your selected photos)
  • Request deletion of any data
  • Contact us with privacy concerns

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the app and on the Play Store listing. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
